Project 4.4 - Miniature Train

IED - April 11, 2016

The objective of this project was to create a model of a virtual train toy for the Lionel Train Company. I designed and modelled the train engine, dining car, and lounge car of our toy train. I also took screenshots of our parts, recorded a few videos of their various features, and worked on the group presentation. Through this project, I learned how to use Autodesk Inventor's appearance menu to customize a part, create curved features using the loft tool, and record videos of Autodesk Inventor. Some challenges I faced during this project included creating a curved train engine and a failing graphics card.
