Paper Toy

Introduction to Engineering Design - May 6, 2016

The objective of this project was to create, design,and model a paper toy for the toy company KaniModel. I was the sole designer and engineer for this project, so I designed, modelled, and built the paper toy. Through this project, I learned how to use Autodesk Inventor's sheet metal functions. Some challenges I faced during this project included the extreme accuracy required to fold the toy and losing my first paper toy.
